# Onboarding Tutorials

## Getting Started
- Log in at `/auth` with your credentials.
- Explore the dashboard and the communication hub.
- Join a video room at `/communication/video` for team onboarding.

## Creating Your First Document
- POST to `/api/documents` with title/slug/content.
- Add versions and comments to collaborate.

## Enrolling in Training
- View modules via `/api/training/modules`.
- Enroll using `/api/training/enrollments`.
- Complete lessons and take assessments.
